Cookie policy

Last updated: March 2026 · TaleBuzzer Ltd · Registered in England & Wales

TaleBuzzer uses only essential cookies required to run the service. We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or cross-site tracking of any kind.

1. What are cookies

Cookies are small text files stored in your browser when you visit a website. TaleBuzzer uses a minimal set of cookies to keep the service working correctly.

2. Cookies we use

We use the following cookies:

  • tb_cookie — Remembers your cookie consent choice. Stored in localStorage. Expires after 1 year.
  • Session cookie — Keeps you logged into your parent account. Expires after 24 hours of inactivity. HttpOnly and Secure.
  • Google Fonts — The font provider may set a performance cookie. No personal data is shared.

3. What we do not use

We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any advertising or tracking technology. We do not track children across websites.

4. Your choices

You can manage or delete cookies through your browser settings at any time. Deleting session cookies will log you out of your account.

5. Contact

Questions: privacy@talebuzzer.com
