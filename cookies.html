Cookie policy

Last updated: March 2026 · TaleBuzzer Ltd · Registered in England & Wales
Governed by the UK Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations 2003 (PECR) and UK GDPR

TaleBuzzer is designed for children. We operate a strict minimal-cookie approach. We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or cross-site profiling of any kind. Children's data is never used for commercial profiling.

1. What are cookies

Cookies are small text files that a website stores on your device when you visit. They allow the site to remember information across pages or between visits. Some cookies are necessary for the service to function. Others are optional and require your consent under UK PECR before they are set.

We also use a browser storage mechanism called localStorage to save your cookie consent choice. This is functionally similar to a cookie and is treated the same way under PECR.

2. Our lawful basis for cookies

Under UK PECR and UK GDPR we rely on two lawful bases for placing cookies:

  • Strictly necessary — no consent required These cookies are set only where they are essential to deliver a service you have specifically requested. Removing them would prevent the site from working. We do not seek consent for these under Regulation 6(4) of PECR.
  • Consent — opt-in required Any non-essential cookie (analytics, performance, marketing) requires your explicit, informed, and freely given consent before it is placed. You can withdraw consent at any time with the same ease as giving it.

4. What we do not use

  • Facebook Pixel or any Meta tracking technology
  • Google Ads or any advertising network cookies
  • Hotjar, FullStory, or any session recording tools
  • TikTok Pixel or any social commerce tracking
  • Cross-site tracking of children or child profiles
  • Fingerprinting or device identification techniques

5. Third-party services

TaleBuzzer uses a small number of third-party services that may set their own cookies or storage items. These are listed below. We have Data Processing Agreements in place with each provider where required by UK GDPR.

  • Google Fonts (Alphabet Inc.) Used to load the typefaces displayed on this site. Google may log your IP address for abuse prevention. No advertising data is processed. Google Fonts privacy FAQ.
  • Formspree (or equivalent email handler) Used to process contact form submissions. Formspree receives your submitted data only. No tracking cookies are set by this integration.
  • Payment processor (Stripe — planned) If you subscribe to a paid plan, Stripe will set its own cookies for fraud prevention and payment processing. Stripe's cookies are subject to Stripe's privacy policy. We will update this policy before payment functionality launches.

6. Your choices and how to manage cookies

You have the following options to control cookies on TaleBuzzer.

  • Accept or reject via the banner When you first visit TaleBuzzer you will see a consent banner. You can accept all optional cookies or reject them. You can change this decision at any time using the preference centre below.
  • Manage preferences on this page Use the preference centre directly below to review and update your choices without leaving this page.
  • Browser settings All modern browsers allow you to view, block, and delete cookies. Deleting the tb_session cookie will log you out of your account. See your browser's help pages for instructions.
  • Opt out of Google Fonts To prevent Google Fonts from loading, you can block fonts.googleapis.com in your browser or use a privacy extension such as uBlock Origin.

8. How long we keep consent records

Your consent choice is stored in localStorage under the key tb_cookie_consent for 12 months. After 12 months we will ask for your consent again. You can clear this at any time by using the "Reject all" button above or by clearing your browser storage.

9. Cookies and children

TaleBuzzer accounts are created by parents or guardians on behalf of children. Children access stories through a PIN-protected child mode and do not interact with cookie consent flows. Cookie consent is collected only from the parent or guardian who holds the account.

We do not use cookies to profile children for commercial purposes. This approach is consistent with the ICO Children's Code (Age Appropriate Design Code) and our obligations under UK GDPR with respect to children's data.

10. Changes to this policy

We review this Cookie Policy whenever we introduce a new technology, update our third-party providers, or receive guidance from the ICO. Material changes that affect your consent will trigger a fresh consent request. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent revision.

If you believe we have breached PECR or UK GDPR, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO): ico.org.uk/make-a-complaint or call 0303 123 1113.
