How It Works

A personalised story in three simple steps

No writing skills needed. No waiting. Just your child's details and a sprinkle of AI magic.

01
👶
Tell us about your child

Enter their name, age, a favourite interest, and choose a story theme. It takes under a minute and no sign-up is needed to preview.

02
✨
AI creates the story

Our AI writes a unique, age-appropriate story with your child as the hero — with vivid characters, an adventure, and a meaningful ending.

03
📖
Read, earn, and explore

Your child reads their story, answers comprehension questions, and earns Bee points to unlock new adventures and accessories.

04
🐝
Watch the habit form

Daily reading streaks, Bee rank promotions, and personalised reminders bring children back every day — without a fight at bedtime.

Features

Everything children need to fall in love with reading

Built for parents, designed for children, and trusted by UK primary schools.

🎭
Your child is always the hero

Every story is written around your child's name, appearance, and interests. Not a name-swap — a truly personalised adventure.

🐝
Bee Reward System

Children earn Bees for reading, answering questions, and maintaining streaks. Ten rank levels from Solitary Bee to Queen Bee.

🌍
Bilingual stories

Generate the same story in English and Spanish side by side. French coming soon. Cultural nuance preserved — not just translated.

📴
Offline reading

Stories download automatically on creation. Perfect for car journeys and places with no internet. Syncs Bees when you reconnect.

🧠
Comprehension questions

Three age-appropriate questions after every story. The only measurable literacy proof point in the product — visible in the parent dashboard.

♿
SEN-friendly mode

OpenDyslexic font, high-contrast display, and natural-voice narration enabled by default for SEN profiles. Accessibility without compromise.

📊
Parent dashboard

See exactly what your child has read, how many words they have encountered, and how their comprehension is improving — week by week.

🔒
Child PIN mode

Your child gets their own locked reading home screen with a 4-digit PIN. No access to billing, settings, or the parent account — ever.

🎁
Story gift cards

Give the gift of a personalised story experience. No account needed to purchase. Perfect for birthdays and celebrations.
