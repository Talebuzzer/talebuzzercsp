How it works

A story in three steps.
Ready in seconds.

Parents set the scene. TaleBuzzer builds the story — illustrated, narrated, and fully personalised.

01
Tell us about your child
Enter their name, age, and favourite things. Takes 30 seconds. Their story stars them as the main character — always.
02
Pick a theme and format
Adventure, mystery, nature, friendship — choose the setting. Text only, illustrated, or read-aloud. English or Spanish.
03
Read, earn, and return
Children earn Bee points for reading and answering questions. Daily streaks make returning tomorrow feel like a reward, not a chore.

Literacy
Comprehension questions
Three age-appropriate questions after every story. Children earn Bees for correct answers. Parents see results. Schools get a measurable literacy proof point.
Accessibility
SEN mode built in
OpenDyslexic font, high-contrast mode, and narration on by default. Designed for every learner from day one.
Independence
Child PIN access
Children enter their own 4-digit PIN to reach their story world. No access to billing or account settings. Daily reading without the parent handover.
Language
English and Spanish
Bilingual stories for the 1.4 million children in UK bilingual homes. Both languages side by side — culturally nuanced, not a literal translation.
